Who I Am

15 years building
AI systems that actually work at scale

I'm not a theorist. I've directed national AI-powered assessments for over 10 million students, represented Brazil at the OECD PISA Governing Board, and built machine learning models recognized by Harvard and UNESCO — achieving 98% accuracy in predicting Brazil's national education index.

Today, based in Sacramento, California, I channel that institutional knowledge into frameworks, books, certifications, and ventures that help organizations govern and implement AI responsibly — without losing speed or innovation.

My work sits at the intersection of three disciplines most professionals treat as separate: artificial intelligence, governance & law, and organizational strategy. The G3 AI Framework is the result of 15 years at that intersection.

The G3 AI Framework

AI without structure
is just expensive chaos

The G3 AI Framework was developed over 15 years of implementing AI in high-stakes environments — national education systems, public institutions, and complex governance structures.

It answers the question no other framework fully addresses: not just how to build AI, but how to govern it, manage it through change, and align it to strategic outcomes that stakeholders can actually see and trust.

Introduced in AI Strategies for Web Development (Packt, UK), the G3 AI Framework is now the foundation of the AIxp certification and the G3AI.global learning platform.
